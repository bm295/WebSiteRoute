# Product Vision: AI-Native Fintech Platform

## Vision statement
Build a secure, compliant, and AI-native fintech platform that helps consumers and small businesses manage money, automate payments, and improve financial decisions.

## Problem statement
Current fintech products often separate:
- transactional banking workflows,
- financial insights,
- and operational support.

Our rewrite will unify these areas using AI agents for personalized insight, fraud triage, operations automation, and developer acceleration.

## Target users
1. **Consumers**: budgeting, saving, card and transfer visibility.
2. **SMBs**: cashflow management, invoices, payroll-like scheduled payouts.
3. **Ops and Risk Teams**: manual review reduction and faster decisioning.

## Core value propositions
- **Transparent money movement** via real-time ledger visibility.
- **Embedded intelligence** with explainable AI suggestions.
- **Fast support resolution** through AI-assisted operations.
- **Compliance by design** with auditable controls.

## Product principles
- Safety before speed.
- Human override for high-risk actions.
- Every AI action is logged, attributable, and reversible where possible.
- API-first and event-driven architecture.

## Success metrics (first 12 months)
- Payment success rate >= 99.5%.
- Fraud loss rate < 0.15% of payment volume.
- Mean support resolution time reduced by 40%.
- AI-assisted engineering throughput increased by 30%.
